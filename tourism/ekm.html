Ernakulam

Cochin is a major port city in God’s own country. Fondly called the Queen of the Arabian Sea, this one of the best places to visit in Kerala that has forever been on every traveler’s bucket list. It is a perfect blend of local flavors and modern ideas, to offer the best to its visitors. The backwaters, beaches, traditional spice markets which are a short drive from Cochin have made their way to list of things to see and experience in Cochin.

Popular tourist attractions: Fort Kochi, Vypeen Beach, Cherai Beach, Andhakaranzhi Beach, Chinese Fishing Nets, Marine Drive, St. FrnacisChurch, Basilica of Santa Cruz, Bolghatty Island, Thrikkara Temple
Best time to visit: July to April
Nearest airport: Cochin International Airport
Nearest railway station: Kochi Railway Junction
