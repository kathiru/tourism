Trivandrum

Thiruvananthapuram is the largest city of the Kerala state. It is also the capital of Kerala and sometime calling by the name Trivandrum. It is the most popular city of the Kerala as well as the India. Trivandrum is the 5th most populous urban city of the Kerala. It is also the major hub for the IT section that contributes mostly software exports. One more famous place in Trivandrum is the Techno Park that is the largest technology park in all over Asia in terms of area. Trivandrum is the most visiting places in Kerala due to the wide area and bigger city of Kerala. Every tourist wants to come there in which some of come for the purpose of shopping, while some of the tourist comes from visiting the place. A large number of people in Trivandrum are the employees that have worked in the IT section companies in Trivandrum, Kerala. There are so many visiting places in Trivandrum that attract tourist so much. One of the popular tourist places is Thiruvanathapuram zoo that has a variety of animals. One more attracting place is the lighthouse beach. It is the visiting beach of the Kerala due to its beauty and awesome look. This beach is popular for the night view. The lighthouse on the seashore is spreading their light in all over direction, but when its light is reflected in the sea water than all the directions is shining with the glittering light. This place is popular for the newly and technological things also it contains the historical things in which Museum is at the top.

Popular tourist attractions: Aruvikkara Dam, Neyyar Dam & Wildlife Sanctuary, Poovar, Koyikkal Palace, Kowdiar Palace, Agasthyakoodam, Kanakakunnu Palace, Anchuthengu Fort, Peppara Dam and Wildlife Sanctuary
Best time to visit: October to February
Nearest airport: Trivandrum International Airport
Nearest railway station: Trivandrum Central Railway Station
