Palakkad

Just like a beautiful picture postcard, Palakkad features most awesome weather, picturesque mountains, sparkling lakes, beautiful dams, lush green forests and majestic temples and forts. This lush greenery makes it another one of the most rejuvenating forest tourist places in Kerala. With vast sprawling paddy estates and tea plantations, Palakkad, one of Kerala’s famous places is aptly known as the ‘granary of Kerala’.

Popular tourist attractions: Silent Valley National Park, Palakkad Fort, Malampuzha Garden and Dam, Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary, Jain Temple and Seethargundu viewpoint
Best time to visit: November to March months are extremely pleasant here.
Nearest airport: Coimbatore Airport
Nearest railway station: Palakkad railway station and Palakkad Town Railway
